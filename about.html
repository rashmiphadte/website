ABOUT US

R&A Builders was established in the year 2010 with a goal to construct high quality, luxurious apartments with competitive pricing in Goa. We deliver with passion, integrity, professionalism and ultimately absolute honesty to give you peace of mind.

Over the last decade, we have been consistently working towards and achieving our objective of providing real estate consisting of apartments, commercial spaces and Portuguese-styled row houses.
